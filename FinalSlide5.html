🧀 Fast Food Grilled Cheeses 🧀

I once had Panera Bread described to me as “overpriced hospital food,” and after having Panera Bread the last time I went to the hospital, the description oddly fit the chain. Hospital food tastes comforting -- especially when it’s something like mac and cheese, soup, or a sandwich, and you’re in a hospital -- so other than the bit about it being “overpriced,” lots of what Panera Bread has to offer isn’t bad by any means.

And then there’s their grilled cheese sandwich.

If you’re a fan of the Try Guys, then you may be familiar with the clip of Keith playing with his grilled cheese sandwich, rubbing it on his face, and saying that while the food doesn’t taste good as a sandwich, it works well as a beauty blender. If you haven’t seen this, here you go:




Everything said here is absolutely true. In my opinion, the bread crust is chewy and hard, and too soft in the middle. There’s also no butter sometimes, from experience, making it seem like a Panera employee just took some bread, slapped some cheese between it, threw the ingredients in a convection oven, and hoped for the best.



The cheese isn’t half bad, but come on. It’s cheese. For a $7 grilled cheese, I’d say it’s not worth it. If you have to go to Panera, get their mac and cheese sandwich. It’s not your classic grilled cheese, but at least it doesn’t taste like it was half-assed.


Overall Rating: 5 out of 10.
